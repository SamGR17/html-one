My favorite childhood memory

My favorite memory from my childhood might be one of the times I went to Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk.

It has been a long time since I last gone there with my brother and sister, I wish I could go again someday.

I remember going on a lot of rides that day, the most memorable rides for me were the Undertow, Shockwave, and the Giant Dipper.

We stayed there until it got dark, seeing all the rides at night was a vibe as I remember it.

It is a shame that you only experience a memory once and don't even realize how precious it becomes until it passes.
